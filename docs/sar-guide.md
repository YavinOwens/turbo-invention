# Filing a Subject Access Request (UK)

Under **UK GDPR Article 15** and the **Data Protection Act 2018**, you have the
right to request a copy of personal data an organisation holds about you. The
ICO is the UK's regulator: https://ico.org.uk/your-data-matters/your-right-of-access/

## Per-platform shortcuts (verify before use — these change)
- **Meta (Facebook, Instagram):** Settings → Your information → Download your information. Choose JSON.
- **Google:** https://takeout.google.com — pick the products you want.
- **X (Twitter):** Settings → Your account → Download an archive of your data.
- **TikTok:** Settings → Account → Download your data.
- **LinkedIn:** Settings → Data privacy → Get a copy of your data.

## What you typically receive
- Posts, comments, messages, reactions
- Profile and contact info
- Ads / inferences and interest categories
- Login history and sessions
- Photos, videos and their metadata

## What you can learn about yourself
- Topics you write about most (term frequency)
- When you're most active (cadence)
- How positive / negative your public voice is (sentiment)
- Latent themes the platform sees you in (topic clusters)
- The advertiser inferences made about you
